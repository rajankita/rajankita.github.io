Namaste!

I am Ankita, a PhD candidate in the Computer Science Department at IIT Delhi. I primarily work on Computer Vision and Trustworthy Machine Learning. In my doctoral thesis, advised by Prof. Chetan Arora, I study the security and privacy vulnerabilities in deep learning-based computer vision systems, including adversarial, backdoor, and model stealing attacks in safety-critical domains such as medical imaging and surveillance. My broader research interests encompass continual learning, and machine unlearning, and computer vision for satellite imaging.

I previously obtained my Masters degree from IIIT Delhi (not the same as IIT Delhi, note the extra "I"), where I worked on optimization and resource allocation in communication systems, advised by Prof. Pravesh Biyani. After completing my Masters and before joining PhD, I also did a stint as a Researcher in the Robotics Lab, Tata Consultancy Services, where I worked on computer vision for robotics and warehousing applications.
